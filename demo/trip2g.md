From spreadsheets, SQL, REST, pre-built metrics and more, there are over a hundred way to connect your team data to Power Metrics. & once connected, you can easily clean up messy, incomplete or complicated data.

Once you connect your data, you can use it to build metrics with common & well understood behaviors, making your data easy to use and combine.

 - Present the most up-to-date data and its history to your team with up-to real-time data refresh.
 - Save hours of manual, repetitive tasks with searchable & accessible data feeds for you and your team.
 - Regardles where your data comes from, once your data in Power Metrics it's standardized so it works the same way in all of your metrics.
